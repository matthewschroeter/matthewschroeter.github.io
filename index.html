About Me

Hello! My name is Matthew Schroeter and I am a 5th year student at Northeastern University working towards a degree in Bioengineering. After working in a bio-inspired robotics lab at Northeastern, I became fascinated with the process of understanding underlying biological phenomena and utilizing it to inspire new systems. In a similar fashion, I seek to utilize my broad educational background and experiences to develop new robotic systems that can meet the challenges of our increasingly complex world. Through this lab I also became interested in electro-mechanical design and controls. I enjoy being hands on, iteratively developing a design and testing it to identify failure modes, then improving the system. In the future, I hope to continually expand my skills in electro-mechanical design and gain experience in implementing a control system.
